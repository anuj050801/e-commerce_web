Online shopping era!!

Online shopping has become increasingly popular in recent years, and for good reason. It's convenient, easy, and you can shop from the comfort of your own home. However, with so many options available, it can be tempting to overspend. Here are five ways you can save money when shopping online.

If you're not in a hurry to make a purchase, wait for sales. Many stores have regular sales throughout the year, especially during holidays and major events like Black Friday. By waiting for a sale, you can often save a significant amount of money.
